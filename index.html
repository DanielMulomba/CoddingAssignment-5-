Ingredients

  • 4 boneless monkfish medallions (140g each)
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, chopped
  • 4 lime leaves, chopped
  • 1 tsp lemon thyme
  • 5 tbsp olive oil
  • A handful of arugula leaves
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 25g caster sugar

Equipment

  • Mixing bowl
  • Knife & chopping board
  • Saucepan
  • Non-stick frying pan
  • Serving plates

Directions

  1. Marinate monkfish for 6 hours with lemongrass, lime, thyme, and oil.
  2. Prepare lemon dressing by boiling zest and mixing with sugar and oil.
  3. Cook fish 4 minutes per side until golden. Serve with dressing and arugula.
